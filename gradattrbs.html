Graduate Attributes

Problem Analysis

CHED

  • Ability to recognize, formulate and solve computing problems.
  • understanding and assessing local and global impacts of computing on society relevant to professional computing practice and subscription to accepted industry standards.
  • Solve complex problems.

Development of Solutions

CHED

  • An ability to design, build, improve, and deploy products that meet client need with realistic constraints.
  • Demonstrate ability to think differently, gather information and develop new ways of understanding.

Modern Tool Usage

CHED

  • LAn ability to use the appropriate techniques, skills and modern computing tools necessary for the practice of being a professional game developer or animator.

Computing Professionalism and Ethics

CHED

  • An understanding of the effects and impacts of entertainment and multimedia computing projects on nature and society, and of their social and ethical responsibilities
  • Ability to have a greater insight into decision-making process.

Individual and Team Work

CHED

  • An ability to work effectively in multi-disciplinary and multi-cultural teams.
  • Work in a multicultural and interdisciplinary team.

Communication

CHED

  • An ability to effectively communicate orally and in writing using English language.
  • Demonstrate effective reading, listening, speaking and writing skills.

Project Management and Finance

CHED

  • An ability to analyze project requirements and to design and implement project prototypes.

Life-long Learning

CHED

  • An ability to engage in life-long learning and an acceptance of the need to keep current of the development in the specific field of specialization.
  • A knowledge of contemporary issues.
  • Demonstrate ability to work on activities associated with self-directed learning projects and build new skills.

Critical and Reflective Analysis

CHED

  • Demonstrate ability to think differently, gather information and develop new ways of understanding.

Creativity and Innovation

CHED

  • An ability to demonstrate innovativeness in their outputs.
  • Generate new ideas.

Judgement and Decision Making

CHED

  • An ability to demonstrate client-centric service.
  • Exhibit leadership capacity and have a greater insight into decision-making process.
